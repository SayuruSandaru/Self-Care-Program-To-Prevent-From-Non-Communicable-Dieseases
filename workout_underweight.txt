• Brisk walking - 30 minutes, 3-4 times a week.
• Cycling - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week.
• Strength training - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week, focus on exercises that target the major muscle groups, such as leg press, lat pull-down, chest press, and leg extension.
• High-intensity interval training (HIIT) - 15-20 minutes, 2-3 times a week.
• Jumping rope - 20 minutes, 2-3 times a week.
• Climbing stairs - 20 minutes, 2-3 times a week.
• Hiking - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week.
• Yoga - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week, focus on poses that improve strength and balance.
• Swimming - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week.
• Kickboxing - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week.
