    Brisk walking - 30 minutes, 3-4 times a week.
    Cycling - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week.
    Swimming - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week.
    Yoga - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week, focus on poses that improve flexibility, balance and breathing.
    Resistance training - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week, focus on exercises that target the major muscle groups, such as leg press, lat pull-down, chest press, and leg extension.
    High-intensity interval training (HIIT) - 15-20 minutes, 2-3 times a week.
    Pilates - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week, focus on core exercises such as the plank, bridge, and bird-dog.
    Dancing - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week, choose a dance style that you enjoy.
    Tai Chi - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week, focus on forms that improve balance, flexibility, and overall fitness.
    Rowing - 30 minutes, 2-3 times a week, focus on proper form and technique to target the major muscle groups.
